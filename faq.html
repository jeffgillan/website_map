Why consider forest thinning at all?


Property Values

Property values are negatively impacted by wildfires with decreases in value anticipated for at least one to three years after a fire.

If your home burns, the cost of rebuilding is significant. The loss of life or personal possessions cannot be valued.

Insurance

Home insurance may be affected by your home's fire risk. You may not qualify for any insurance if your home does not have enough defensible space immediately around it.
