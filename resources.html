Resources

This work uses publicly-available data.

Data are owned by their respective entities. Where not explicitly identified, refer to the source entity for (re)use agreements.


Lidar

Occupied aircraft Light Detection and Range (lidar) data from the United States Geological Survey (USGS) 3DEP program are available for the Jemez Mountains. Those data were collected in 2016 and 2018.

Lidar data from 2012 collected by United States Forest Service (USFS) Collaborative Forest Landscape Restoration Program: Southwest Jemez are available upon request to the USFS R3 GIS office.

Research lidar data from 2012 and 2010 collected by the National Science Foundation are available from OpenTopography.org

SfM

Structure from Motion (SfM) photogrammetry data from small unmanned aerial systems, i.e. drones, were collected as part of this project over a limited area
